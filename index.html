  • Click the start game button and start kicking the ball by pressing space bar within 5 seconds.
  • If you stayed longer, it will be the turn of your opponent/opponents.
  • Any player that scores 10 goals will be the winner!
  • If both players score 10 after taking all their turns, it will be a tie.
  • The game can be reset if any player's score is less than or equal to 5. If not, the game shall continue to the end.
  • If any player wants to quit the game before the end or without reset, please know that the opponent is the winner.
  • Once a game is over, click the "Next Level" button if you need to increase speed of moving goal
